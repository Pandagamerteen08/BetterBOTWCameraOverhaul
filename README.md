# Overview

**Better Breath of the Wild (BOTW) Camera Overhaul** is a complete enhancement and expansion of *The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild's* camera system.

The goal of this project is not to replace the identity of Breath of the Wild's camera. Nintendo created one of the most carefully designed third-person cameras in modern gaming, balancing exploration, combat, traversal, and cinematic presentation.

Instead, this project improves and expands that foundation.

Better BOTW Camera Overhaul provides players with complete control over nearly every aspect of the camera while preserving the original Breath of the Wild feeling.

From simple quality-of-life improvements such as disabling camera shake and adjusting distance, to advanced features such as first-person mode, free camera controls, cinematic presets, and full camera customization, this mod transforms the camera into a fully configurable system.

# Project Philosophy

## Preserve the Original Experience

The default configuration of Better BOTW Camera Overhaul is designed to feel like the original game.

Players who prefer Nintendo's original camera can use the included:

This preset keeps the familiar Breath of the Wild camera style while adding:

- Improved collision handling
- Optional camera shake controls
- Better accessibility options
- More customization
- Smoother transitions

# Why This Mod Exists

Breath of the Wild's camera is already excellent, but there are areas where players have requested improvements:

- Limited camera customization
- No built-in camera shake control
- No FOV adjustment
- Limited accessibility options
- Fixed camera behavior between gameplay states
- No first-person exploration option
- No advanced photography tools

Better BOTW Camera Overhaul addresses these limitations by creating a complete camera framework.

# Camera Shake Overhaul

## Complete Camera Shake Control

Breath of the Wild includes camera shake during:

- Explosions
- Enemy attacks
- Environmental events
- Impacts
- Certain abilities
- Scripted moments

Better BOTW Camera Overhaul adds full control.

Options:

### Vanilla

Uses the original Nintendo camera shake behavior.

### Reduced

Reduces shake intensity.

### Minimal

Allows small cinematic movement while reducing discomfort.

### Disabled

Completely removes camera shake.

### Custom

Allows users to manually configure intensity.
