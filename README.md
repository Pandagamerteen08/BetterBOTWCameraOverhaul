# 1. Project Identity

## Better Breath of the Wild (BOTW) Camera Overhaul

**Better Breath of the Wild (BOTW) Camera Overhaul** is a complete camera enhancement framework for **The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild** designed to improve, expand, and modernize the game's camera system while preserving the original design philosophy created by Nintendo.

This project is not designed to replace Breath of the Wild's identity.

Nintendo created one of the most carefully designed third-person cameras in modern gaming, balancing:

- Exploration
- Combat
- Traversal
- Environmental awareness
- Cinematic presentation

Instead, Better BOTW Camera Overhaul expands that foundation by giving players complete control over their camera experience.

The goal is simple:

> Keep the feeling of Breath of the Wild while removing the limitations of the original camera system.

---

# Project Mission Statement

Better BOTW Camera Overhaul exists to provide:

- Complete camera customization
- Improved accessibility
- Better cinematic control
- Advanced photography tools
- Creator-focused camera systems
- A flexible camera framework for the Breath of the Wild modding community

Players should be able to experience Hyrule from their preferred perspective.

Whether that means:

- A traditional Nintendo-style camera
- A cinematic exploration camera
- A first-person adventure
- A content creator camera
- A competitive combat camera

the choice belongs to the player.

# 7. Project Goals and Non-Goals

# Project Goals

Better BOTW Camera Overhaul aims to:

## Improve Camera Customization

Provide players with complete control over:

- Camera distance
- Camera height
- Field of View
- Camera movement
- Camera smoothing
- Camera behavior

---

## Preserve Breath of the Wild's Identity

The mod should always feel like Breath of the Wild.

The goal is:

"Better, not different."

The original camera design remains the foundation.

---

## Improve Accessibility

Provide options for players who experience discomfort from camera movement.

Accessibility features include:

- Camera shake removal
- Reduced camera movement
- Adjustable sensitivity
- Reduced acceleration
- Custom FOV limits

---

## Support Content Creators

Enable creators to make:

- Cinematic videos
- YouTube content
- Screenshots
- Trailers
- Machinima-style scenes

---

## Create a Camera Framework

The project is designed as a framework instead of a single camera tweak.

This allows:

- Future expansion
- Community contributions
- Additional camera states
- Creator tools

---

# Non-Goals

Better BOTW Camera Overhaul does not aim to:

## Replace Gameplay Mechanics

This project will not modify:

- Combat systems
- Weapons
- Enemy behavior
- Movement mechanics
- Physics systems

---

## Change Breath of the Wild's Visual Style

The project does not aim to:

- Replace textures
- Modify lighting
- Change graphics
- Add visual effects unrelated to cameras

---

## Force Players Into One Camera Style

The mod will never require:

- First-person mode
- Cinematic mode
- Free camera mode

All advanced features remain optional.

Better BOTW Camera Overhaul introduces complete FOV control.

Players can configure separate FOV values for every camera state.

Examples:

| Camera State | Example FOV |
|---|---:|
| Walking | 70° |
| Sprinting | 80° |
| Horse Riding | 85° |
| Gliding | 100° |
| First Person | 90° |

# FOV Range

45°

Default:

Maximum:

# Camera Distance Control

Players can adjust how far the camera sits from Link.

Options:

- Close
- Default
- Far
- Custom

Uses:

- Exploration
- Combat visibility
- Cinematic shots
- Accessibility

---

# Camera Height Control

Allows vertical camera adjustment.

Higher camera positions:

- Increase visibility
- Improve world awareness

Lower camera positions:

- Create cinematic perspectives
- Provide closer gameplay views

---

# Camera Offset System

Allows players to move the camera position.

Settings:

- Horizontal offset
- Vertical offset
- Shoulder position
- Combat offset
